Code for Palestine is a three year-long program, beginning the summer before 10th grade and concluding after 12th grade. Each year has many sessions but is kicked off by a two-week camp during the summer in Jericho in Palestine. Volunteer undergraduate instructors from Stanford University have served as the primary teachers during the two-week summer camp. During the sessions the best in Palestine have worked as teachers. This program is very important because many of students learn progrmming and other skills or improve their skills in programming
